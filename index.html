The Impact We're Making
Family Sponsored

We have sponsored a family from Syria looking for new beginnings in Canada. With your help, we will be able to help more families call Canada their home!

30K Donated

With the help of 85 donors and fundraisers, we have raised over $30,000 to 360's cause. Help us reach our goal of 36K!

Raising Awareness

Through various fundraisers and events, we are continuing to raise awareness to the devastating crisis.

Awarded by RBC and Sorompist International

360 has been recognized by the Royal Bank of Canada and Sorompist International, a global volunteer organization to improve the lives of women and girls worldwide, for our mission and cause!
